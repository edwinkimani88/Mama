🔍 Watafuta Kazi

Find Work in 4 Simple Steps

Your profile is your CV. No fancy papers needed — just you, your skills and your availability.

1

Create Your Free Profile

Add your photo, name, age, location (e.g. Nairobi) and what work you do. No long essay. Just the basics.

2

Browse Available Jobs

See real jobs posted near you — house cleaning, cooking, babysitting, gardening, and more. Filter by category.

3

Pay a Small Fee to Unlock

When you find a job you want, pay a small once-off access fee to unlock the job poster's phone number. The fee varies by job salary level — from as low as KSh 100 for small kibarua up to KSh 2,000 for high-paying monthly positions.

4

Contact & Secure the Job

Reach out directly to the poster. Agree on your terms. Show up and work. It's that simple.

❓ FAQs

Common Questions

💰 How much does it cost to post a job?

Posting a job is completely free. You never pay anything to list your job or to be contacted by workers.

🔓 How much do workers pay to unlock a job?

The access fee varies based on the job's salary level — keeping it proportionate and fair:

  • 🟢 Small kibarua (under KSh 1,000) → KSh 100
  • 🟡 Daily / weekly jobs → KSh 200
  • 🟠 Monthly mid-range (KSh 5K–15K) → KSh 1,000
  • 🔴 Monthly KSh 15,000+ → KSh 2,000

This ensures only genuinely interested workers reach you — no wasted time for anyone.

📸 Do I need a photo to apply for jobs?

We strongly encourage a clear, honest profile photo. It helps job posters trust you and improves your chances of getting hired. It's not mandatory but highly recommended.

🇰🇪 What areas does Mama Network cover?

We currently focus on Nairobi and surrounding areas — Kikuyu, Kiambu, Ruaka, Ngong, Machakos — and we're growing quickly across Kenya.

⭐ What is "Previously Hired"?

When a job poster confirms that you completed a job, your profile gets a "Previously Hired" badge. Over time this builds your reputation and makes it easier to get more jobs.
